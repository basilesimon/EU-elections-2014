The "number of mentions in the media" was calculated from April 1st, 2014, to election day.
We aggregated all the content content from 40+ major news organisations in the UK and used semantic analysis to extract conceptual entities, e.g. "Nigel Farage", or "The Conservative Party", for example.
The publishers collection ranges from national publications (The Guardian, Sky News, The Financial Times, The Huffington Post...) to local newspapers (Yorkshire Evening Post, Birmingham Post, Belfast Telegraph, The Evening Standard).
